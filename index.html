Telegram is known for its strong focus on security and privacy. The app uses end-to-end encryption to ensure that messages can only be read by the sender and the recipient. WHAT IS TELEGRAM?

Telegram is a multi-platform messaging service. It is known for its emphasis on security and speed. Telegram allows users to send messages, photos, videos, and other files to others and create group chats of up to 200,000 members. You can use it on various platforms, including iOS, Android, and desktop (Windows and OSX).


WHAT IS TELEGRAM APP?

It is free for all and enjoys a high position among other messenger apps. Telegram is fast, secure, and has many unique features.
